What changed and why

The heart of the tool. Every material change gets a plain-English reason and a methodology note, using only the numbers passed in and DEFRA's own change notes. Where the notes are silent, it says so rather than inventing a reason.

Electricity generated · Electricity: UK
Scope 2 0.17700 → 0.13096 kg CO₂e ▼ 26.0%
Why it changed  ·  offline extract
The factor fell 26.0% (0.177 → 0.13096). Official note: "Revision to the calculation method for UK electricity (affected tabs: UK electricity; Transmission and distribution; indirect knock-on effects on other factors that include electricity including Rail, xEVs and Homeworking). A revised methodology has been introduced: the previous approach divided total electricity-related emissions from the UK Greenhouse Gas Inventory by total generation, which introduced a two-year lag…"
Methodology note
Reported under the same GHG Protocol scope classification; the change is a factor-version update, so year-over-year comparisons should note the DEFRA release year (ISO 14064 comparability).
▼ Target impact: this factor decreased, easing the product footprint.
